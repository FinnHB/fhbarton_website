About me

Hi! You can call me Finn.

I'm an economist currently based in Brussels, interested in spatial components of economics & policy making, with a special emphasis on the environment. Explaining the outcomes of economic models can often be difficult, but a well-designed visualisation can often provide some unique insights. On this site, I aim to share my thoughts on topics I find interesting in the blog.

Currently, I am working as an economic modeller at Cambridge Econometrics where I do scenario modelling and maintain the E3ME global macroeconometric model. Previously worked as a research assistant at the NOVA School for Business and Economics in Portugal, trying to better understand how proximity and structural characteristics of parking facilities affect competition.

Feel free to drop me a line at finn_henrik@hotmail.com, check out my CV or LinkedIn. You can also view my code on github or find me on Twitter.
